Hi & Welcome

Silvina is an Argentine tango dancer based in Wellington, New Zealand, and she is excited to help you dance tango with ease!

Silvina's classes are recommended for both followers and leaders who want to work on their fundamentals: balance, walk, and musicality.

You can book a session via the calendar link below, and afterwards you will receive an email with the details (the cost of the class, the studio location, etc). Please note that these classes are based in Wellington, New Zealand.
