Welcome to Tango Ease, I'm so happy you are here!

Tango Ease is a small tango studio in Wellington, New Zealand. It's run by me, Silvina Pugliese, and I offer dance classes and curated tango wear.

I have a deep love for tango since my first tango class in Mar del Plata in 2011, and it's a big privilege to be part of the New Zealand tango community and be of help in your tango journey.

Feel free to book a lesson with me, and check out my free resources. Looking forward to seeing you at milongas too!

In 2017, Silvina had the pleasure of performing with maestro Ricardo Viqueira at the NZ Tango Festival.
